Typography

Most interactive products are built with syndication in mind. From research conducted, column widths for embedding content can vary from 592px wide on the BBC, 620px wide on the Guardian to 700px wide on the ONS website for example (desktop views).

We need to design our interactive products with syndication and the ONS website in mind.

Break points on the ONS website are:

  • Desktop (992px and up)
  • Tablet (768px to 991px)
  • Mobile (up to 767px) are 100% wide

Break points for interactive content

To make sure the design of our interactive products are optimised for different devices, we need consider additional/different break points for our content. These should be consistent on all interactive products.

  • 590px to 767px
  • 321px to 589px
  • 320px
